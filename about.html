About Angel

Introducing Angel, the esteemed proprietor of Bayside F&B Construction Corp, a construction company with an impressive two-decade legacy. Boasting 20 years of hands-on experience in the industry, Angel has carved a path marked by dedication and a meticulous approach to construction.

In the realm of Bayside F&B Construction Corp, Angel envisions more than just erecting structures; it's about crafting enduring spaces that transcend expectations. Setting a standard of excellence, Angel is committed to delivering projects that not only meet but surpass client aspirations.
